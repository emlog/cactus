仙人掌 - 快速翻译、AI解读
Quick translation, AI interpretation

仙人掌 - 快速翻译解读 是一款对选中文字快速进行翻译、AI解读总结的小工具。

Cactus - Quick Translation and Interpretation

Cactus - Quick Translation and Interpretation is a small tool that quickly translates and summarizes selected text using AI.


【主要功能】

智能助力：通过和人工智能扮演的助理交流，获得帮助和鼓励。

阅读练习：阅读人工智能生成的英语阅读素材，联系英语阅读能力，积累词汇。

英语听力：收听由Azure TTS 服务合成的逼近真人的语音朗读，提高英语听力能力。

写作助手：整理你的文档，修正其中的错误，优化文法。帮助你提炼更具吸引力的标题。

【Main Features】

Intelligent Assistance: Obtain help and encouragement through communication with an AI assistant.

Reading Practice: Read English reading materials generated by artificial intelligence to practice English reading skills and accumulate vocabulary.

English Listening: Listen to speech synthesized by Azure TTS service that closely resembles human voice to improve English listening skills.

Writing Assistant: Organize your documents, correct errors, and optimize grammar. Help you refine more attractive titles.
